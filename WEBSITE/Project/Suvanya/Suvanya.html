PROJECT SUMMARY:

Suvanya is dedicated to providing natural, wholesome food products like jaggery and turmeric that promote health and well-being. Their focus is on delivering authentic, high-quality organic foods that people can trust, helping them make healthier choices every day.

Through the brand identity, the goal was to create a visual language that feels natural, modern, and approachable, making it easy for consumers to connect with the brand and embrace a healthier lifestyle.
