Luxe Lustre

PROJECT SUMMARY


Luxe Lustre is more than a makeup studio — it's a space where beauty feels effortless and confidence comes naturally. Every brushstroke is about celebrating individuality, bringing out each person's unique glow with care and creativity.

The brand identity was designed to reflect that same feeling of warmth and sophistication. Soft tones, elegant details, and modern simplicity come together to capture the heart of Luxe Lustre — a brand that helps people see the beauty that's already theirs.

Client

Luxe Lustre

Year

2025

Services

Logo Design, Brand Identity

The Challenge

Luxury that feels personal, not intimidating

Makeup studios in the premium segment often lean on cold, minimal aesthetics that feel aspirational but distant. Luxe Lustre needed to communicate high-end quality while still feeling warm and welcoming — a space where clients feel celebrated, not judged.

The Approach

Warmth within elegance

The identity was built around soft, warm tones paired with refined typography — avoiding the stark black-and-white of typical luxury brands. Every design decision was made to feel approachable and feminine while maintaining a premium, polished quality throughout.

The Outcome

A premium identity system clients connect with

Delivered a complete brand system: logo suite, colour palette, typography hierarchy, illustrations, and brand guidelines. The identity works across studio signage, digital platforms, and marketing collateral — cohesive at every touchpoint.
