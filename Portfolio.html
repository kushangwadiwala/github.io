About Kushang Wadiwala
The designer who makes ideas speak

I’m a graphic designer passionate about transforming ideas into visuals that truly communicate. From sleek logos to attention-grabbing social content and complete brand identities, I combine creativity with clarity to craft designs that leave an impact.

Design, for me, goes beyond aesthetics—it’s about telling stories, connecting with people, and creating memorable experiences. Based in Surat, Gujarat, and holding a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering, I’ve spent the last 3.5 years honing my skills through freelance projects and personal experiments that push the boundaries of my creativity.

Every project is an opportunity to explore, learn, and bring ideas to life in meaningful ways.

  • Location: Surat, Gujarat, India
  • Available for: Freelance, part-time, contract
  • Skills: Adobe Illustrator, Canva, Figma
